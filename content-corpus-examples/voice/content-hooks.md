# Content Hooks

Proven opening patterns that grab attention. The AI selects from these when writing articles.

## Contrarian Statement
- "Everything you've been told about [topic] is backwards."
- "[Common practice] is the reason your [metric] is stuck."

## Specific Number + Unexpected Result
- "I [specific action] and [unexpected metric] happened."
- "[Number] [things] that [surprising outcome]."

## Direct Challenge
- "Stop [common behavior]. Here's what to do instead."
- "If you're still [outdated practice], you're leaving [value] on the table."

## Story Opening
- "Last [time period], I [specific action]. Here's what happened."
- "A client came to me with [problem]. Within [timeframe], [result]."

## Question Hook
- "What if [assumption] was completely wrong?"
- "Why do [audience] keep [frustrating pattern]?"
