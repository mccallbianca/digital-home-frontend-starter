# Tone Examples

Real examples of the brand voice in action. The AI uses these to calibrate tone.

## Blog Post Opening (Authoritative + Provocative)
> [Write 2-3 example openings in your voice. These should be real sentences you've written or would write.]
>
> Example: "I fired my marketing agency last Tuesday. Not because they were bad — because they were unnecessary."

## Email (Direct + Personal)
> [Write 2-3 example email snippets in your voice.]
>
> Example: "Quick question: if your website disappeared tomorrow, would your business survive? Most can't answer that honestly."

## Social Post (Punchy)
> [Write 2-3 example social posts in your voice.]
>
> Example: "You don't have a marketing problem. You have an infrastructure problem wearing a marketing costume."

## Community Message (Warm + Real)
> [Write 2-3 example community messages in your voice.]
>
> Example: "New here? Welcome. The only rule: share what you're actually building, not what you think sounds impressive."
